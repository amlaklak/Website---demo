About Me

Who Is Charles Mlaklak?

I am Charles Mlaklak, a passionate human development mentor based in Lihir Island, New Ireland Province, Papua New Guinea. My mission is simple: to walk alongside students and adults as they discover their God-given potential.

Growing up in PNG, I understand the unique challenges our communities face — from limited access to quality education, to navigating personal and professional growth in a rapidly changing world. These experiences drove me to become the mentor I wish I had.

Whether you're a student trying to find direction, or an adult seeking personal development and life skills, I offer guidance grounded in real experience, cultural understanding, and a deep commitment to seeing Lihir and New Ireland thrive.

My Mission

To equip individuals in Lihir and across Papua New Guinea with the mindset, tools, and confidence to Learn, Grow, and Succeed — regardless of their background.

My Vision

A Lihir and New Ireland where every student graduates with purpose, and every adult lives a life of fulfillment, dignity, and continuous self-improvement.

Local Impact

Proudly serving the Lihir Island community — understanding PNG's unique cultural context to deliver mentorship that truly resonates and creates lasting change.

My Story

My Journey

From the shores of Lihir Island to becoming a dedicated mentor — here's how my path unfolded.

Early Beginnings

Roots in Lihir Island

Born and raised in Lihir, New Ireland Province, Papua New Guinea, I grew up in a close-knit community shaped by tradition, resilience, and a deep desire to see our people succeed. Early on, I recognised the untapped potential in those around me.

Discovery

Discovering Human Development

I discovered my passion for human development through personal struggles and triumphs. I learned that with the right guidance, resources, and mindset, every individual — regardless of background — can rise above their circumstances and build a meaningful life.

Growth

Starting to Mentor

I began mentoring students and adults in Lihir informally — sharing what I had learned about goal setting, personal discipline, and living purposefully. The transformation I witnessed in others fuelled my commitment to formalise this work.

Today

Learn. Grow. Succeed.

Today, I serve the Lihir and New Ireland community as a dedicated Human Development Mentor, conducting workshops, coaching sessions, and community talks. My goal is to see PNG's next generation rise with confidence, skills, and purpose. This is just the beginning.
